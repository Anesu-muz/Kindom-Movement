About Us

The Fuel for Our Mission

Kingdom Movement ignites passionate students like YOU to discover the true gospel and transform your campus. We're a welcoming community open to anyone seeking faith, friendship, and making a real difference.

Our 3 main cores:

This is our broad mission statement: Kingdom Movement is a community of students dedicated to putting the life, teachings, and character of Jesus into practice.

We work to practice this life through our discussion-based small groups on campus. Together we learn to practice and grow in the life Jesus has to offer.

We believe that through this intentional community, we really can transform the world

Join us and change the world, one heart at a time!

Let's discuss & discover together.

Feeling lost in the crowd? Grab a seat in a D-Group! These dynamic groups offer a place to connect with like-minded people, explore faith, and discover your purpose on campus. It's more than just attending another meeting - it's about building friendships, finding support, and making a real difference. No matter where you're at in your journey, there's a D-Group ready to welcome you in. Ready to find your place? Join us and see what you've been missing!

Our Events

  • Worship Afternoon

    Kingdom Movement ignites a vibrant worship afternoon where you can connect with God and others, discover your calling, and fuel your spirit. Join us for an afternoon filled with powerful music, inspiring speakers, and a welcoming community. Don't just exist, thrive! See you there!

  • Games day

    Tired of the same old routine? Kingdom Movement is bringing the fun with an Epic Games Day! Make new friends, laugh until you cry, and discover how faith can be an adventure!. We'll have awesome games, epic challenges, and a chance to connect with God in a whole new way. Ready to play? Join the fun!

  • Prayer Session

    Kingdom Movement invites you to a powerful prayer session where you can lift your voice to God, connect with others in shared faith, and discover strength in community. Leave your burdens at the door and experience the peace and power of prayer. Join us and see what a difference prayer can make!
